Twin Cities Marathon

Using D3.js to visualize the 2017 Twin Cities Marathon

To begin learning the D3.js visualization library, I've illustrated the race results of the 2017 Twin Cities marathon. The Twin Cities Marathon is a 26.2 mile race that takes place in early October every year. The race course starts in downtown Minneapolis, circles many of the cities lakes, and continues along the Mississippi river until reaching the finish line at the state capitol in St. Paul.


In 2017, a total of 7,487 runners completed the Twin Cities marathon.

The average time to complete the entire 26.2 mile course was 4 hours, 24 minutes and 6 seconds.

There were 3396 female finishers with an average time of 4 hours, 35 minutes and 28 seconds.

There were 4091 male finishers with an average time of 4 hours, 14 minutes and 40 seconds.
